Would you like to share your dogs story?

Copy and paste these questions and email the completed story to jamaicaplaindogs at gmail dot com.

You can also access these questions on Google drive.

Please feel free to answer as many or as few as you would like.

You can really write anything you want in any style.

If you would like to do a family photoshoot for the story, or photos of just your dog, we can schedule a time after you submit the story. You will be able to choose what photos will be posted publicly.

I. Intro

Your dog's breed / age / how old was your dog when you got them? (any Doggy DNA test results if a mixed breed?)

What are some of your dogs nicknames?

Tell me about what makes your dog special, something like their silliness, playfulness, athleticism, intelligence, or something that surprises and delights you.

What would you most like to say about your dog?

What do you know about your dog's life before they joined yours?

II. Personality

How would you describe some of your dog's most prominent personality traits?

How does your dog's personality suit you, and how much are you alike? Are there attributes about your dog that you wish you had more of?

What were they like as a puppy, and how have they changed over time?

What makes your dog happy?

Does your dog have a certain play style? - (ball/retriever/frisbee - solo or pack play).

III. You and your dog

How did your life change when you got your dog?

What do you see when you look at your dog?

Did you grow up with dogs? How is it different now?

IV. Dog Pack and Community

Does your dog have a pack and or a best friend?

What does having a dog pack/dog parent community mean to you?
